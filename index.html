Rules

1.Roll the dice.
2.You can roll the dice any no of times.Everytime you roll the dice,the number on the dice gets added to the current score.
3.When you click hold button ,your current score gets added to the total score and the turn goes to the next player.
4.If you roll '1',the current score becomes 0 and turn goes to next player.
5.If you roll '6' twice ,your total score becomes '0' and turn goes to next player.
6.You can set the maximum score to win.Select the max score and click on the "Tick button" to set the max score.
Note:By default,Max score is set to 100 and You cannot change the maximum score while playing.
7.To start a new game ,click on the button "New game".
